Détermination de la valeur actuelle ($C$)

\[\begin{aligned} A & = C(1+i)^n \hspace{4cm} \Big| \cdot (1+i)^{-n} \\ C & = A(1+i)^{-n} \end{aligned} \]

Recherche du taux de placement ($i$)

\[\begin{aligned} A & = C(1+i)^n \hspace{4cm} \Big| \cdot C^{-1} \\ (1+i)^n & = \frac{A}{C} \hspace{4cm} \Big| \sqrt[n]{\cdot} \\ 1+i & = \sqrt[n]{\frac{A}{C}} \hspace{4cm} \Big| -1 \\ i & = \sqrt[n]{\frac{A}{C}} - 1 \end{aligned} \]

Recherche du temps de placement ($n$)

\[\begin{aligned} A & = C(1+i)^n \hspace{4cm} \Big| \cdot C^{-1} \\ (1+i)^n & = \frac{A}{C} \hspace{4cm} \Big| \log(\cdot) \\ n \cdot \log(1+i) & = \log \frac{A}{C} \hspace{4cm} \Big| \div \log(1+i) \\ n & = \frac{ \log \frac{A}{C} }{ \log(1+i) } \end{aligned} \]
